# Privacy Policy
Effective date: 2026-01-14

This Privacy Policy explains how the Mobile Pension Forecaster app (the "App") handles data. If you have questions, contact [support email].

## Overview
- The App runs pension calculations locally on your device.
- We do not operate backend servers for the App.
- We request non-personalized ads from Google AdMob.

## Information We Collect
### Data you provide
- Financial and retirement planning inputs you enter in the App.
- This data is stored locally on your device using app storage and is not transmitted to us.

### Automatically collected data
- When ads are shown, Google AdMob may collect device and usage data (such as device identifiers, IP address, approximate location, app information, and ad interactions) to deliver and measure ads.
- We do not receive this data directly.

### Reports and exports
- When you export or share reports, files are generated on your device and shared only if you choose to do so.

## How We Use Data
- Run simulations and show results in the App.
- Save your settings locally for convenience.
- Display ads and measure performance via AdMob.

## Sharing
- We do not sell your personal information.
- We share data only with:
  - Google AdMob for ad serving and measurement.
  - Apps or services you choose when you share exported files.

## Data Retention
- Local data remains on your device until you delete it, reset the App, or uninstall.
- Exported files remain until you delete them.

## Your Choices
- You can clear local data by using any in-app reset options or uninstalling the App.
- You can limit ad tracking or reset your advertising ID in your device settings.
- You can choose not to share exported files.

## Your Rights
- **Access**: All your data is visible within the App.
- **Correction**: You can edit any information at any time.
- **Deletion**: You can reset to defaults or uninstall the App to delete all data.
- **Portability**: You can export your forecasts as PDF reports.

## Children's Privacy
- The App is not directed to children under 13.
- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information, contact us.

## Third-Party Services
- Google AdMob: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads
- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Changes
We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the effective date above and may provide notice in the App or store listing.
